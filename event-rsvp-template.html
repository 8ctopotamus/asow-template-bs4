ABOUT

Advance School of Wrestling provides specialized wrestling training and techniques to wrestlers of all ages in the Madison, WI area. Our programs help athletes reach their full potential with wrestling technique training, wrestling fundamentals, strength and conditioning, mental development and nutrition. Our coaches bring 30+ years of expertise that guides athletes to a new level.
